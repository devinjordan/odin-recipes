Pork Chops and Sweet & Sour Peppers

The perfect combination of sweet, savory, and sour. This dish checks all the boxes. The perfect sear on your pork, alongside tender, yet crunchy vegetables is something worth drooling over.

Ingredients

  • Pork Chops, Bone-In (Pork tenderloin is a fine substitution)
  • Onion, Sweet or Yellow
  • Red, Yellow, Green Bell Peppers
  • Fresh Basil
  • Red Wine Vinegar
  • Sugar

Steps

  • Utilize whatever preferred method you'd like for cooking the pork. I prefer either stove-top sear with a finish in the oven, or sous vide with a sear to finish.
  • Slice the onion and peppers into long, thin strips. Place them into a bowl and coat with salt. Do not rinse them as this will ruin the sautee.
  • Sautee the peppers and onions in a large skillet, pre-warmed with 1 tbsp. of olive oil and a spoonful of sugar.
  • Add the red wine vinegar, fresh basil, and another tbsp of olive oil. Allow this to stew until the onions and peppers are tender, then remove from heat.
  • Plate this with your pork chop(s) and enjoy!
