Pasta Alla Vodka

Description

This recipe is a classic! The sauce is rustic and creamy, with a warmth to it (thanks to the vodka!). It pairs very well with sliced or ground italian sausage to give it some extra protein. You'll likely want to use a round variety of pasta (think rotini or penne).

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. Penne or Rotini Pasta
  • 20 oz. Can of peeled tomatoes (San Marzano preferred)
  • Italian Seasaonings (Oregano, Sage, Rosemary, Basil, Thyme, Red Pepper Flakes)
  • 1/2 to 3/4 lbs. Italian Sausage (sliced or ground)
  • Garlic Toast (Optional)
  • Parmesan (Parmigiano Reggiano preferred)

Steps

  1. Bring a pot of salted water to a boil. The water should be salted to taste. Follow the instructions on the box to cook the pasta.
  2. Cook the italian sausage in a pan with 1 tbsp. of olive oil. Set aside for later.
  3. In a large saucepan, combine the peeled tomatoes, tomato paste (if desired), and seasonings to taste. When the sauce has reduced by 1/4, add the italian sausage (if ground) to make a meaty sauce.
  4. When the pasta is cooked to desired doneness, plate and top with sauce. Garnish with grated parmesan and fresh ground pepper.
